How To Use Our On-the-Go,From Head to Toe-Portable Essentials Set

Body Wash
Start your shower with Zest Pure Natural Body Wash. Squeeze a small amount onto a loofah or your hands, then gently lather over your body. Rinse thoroughly to leave your skin feeling clean and refreshed. The natural ingredients will hydrate your skin, leaving it soft and nourished.
Shampoo
Apply Zest Pure Natural Shampoo to wet hair, massaging it into your scalp to create a rich lather. This natural formula will cleanse your hair, removing dirt and excess oils without stripping its natural moisture. Rinse thoroughly to enjoy fresh, clean hair.
Conditioner
After shampooing, apply *Zest Pure Natural Conditioner* evenly through the ends of your hair. Let it sit for 2-3 minutes to deeply nourish and hydrate your hair. Rinse well, and enjoy soft, smooth hair that feels healthy and vibrant.
